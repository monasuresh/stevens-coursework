About Monica Suresh

Biography

I am Monica Suresh and I was born in Winchester, Massachusetts. I come from an Indian heritage, with both my parents originating from India. As an only child, my early interests revolved around solving jigsaw puzzles and immersing myself in the world of books. My family's journey took us from Florida, where I resided between the ages of 3 and 7, back to Massachusetts when I turned 7. During high school, I embarked on a new academic adventure by attending a preparatory school. It was during my high school years that I first delved into the captivating realm of programming. The spark was ignited when I enrolled in a programming course during one of my summer breaks, marking the beginning of my fascination with the world of coding and computer science.

Initially, my career path did not lead me towards computer science. Instead, I aspired to follow in the footsteps of my uncle, who had a successful career as a product manager. To set out on this trajectory, I enrolled in Bentley University. During my time at Bentley University, I pursued a major in Computer Information Systems, aligning my academic journey with the skills needed for the world of business and technology. However, upon completing my undergraduate studies, my interests began to shift away from product management. I reignited my curiosity for programming, specifically in the realm of Java. This led me to a pivotal decision to pursue a Master's degree in Computer Science. In this pursuit, I uncovered the Stevens Online MSCS program, a perfect fit for my aspirations. I applied for the program, and with great satisfaction, I was admitted, opening the door to a future in computer science.
